Introducing Take a Seat

By Double Double Mafia



A web system created for you to create, sell and buy events



CREATE YOUR EVENT JUST AS IT IS


For complexes or simple events, Take a Seat can simplify your organization, and make you focus on the physical preparation of it!


Give specific information about your event. Chose a date, say when it starts, and when your customers can already get into it. Show sponsors. With Take a Seat, you can even upload a structure of your event, showing your customers the location of each sector.

TAKE A SEAT TAKES CARE OF YOUR TICKETS


It doesn't matter if you are selling or buying tickets. With take a seat and our mobile app, just bring the QR Code to the entrance, and you are good to go!


In order to help you to organize your event better, Take a Seat presents a concept of QR Code ticket. Your customer doesn't need to bring a paper with all the information. Just print your screen with the QR Code at the Purchased Events session, and you can validate your customer's ticket at the entrance of your event

BUY THE WAY YOU LIKE


Browse and buy easy. With Take a Seat, you have dozens of events of your interest, easily reachable.


With our system, you can login, manage your profile, browse through events that may interest you, and buy tickets for wherever you want to go! Easy and simple! You can also take a look at the creator of the event, to see other events created by that person!
